# Statistics — A/B and Bayesian

## TL;DR

- **Frequentist:** p-values misread easily; pre-register metrics.
- **Bayesian:** useful for updating belief with sparse data; pick priors carefully.

## Pitfalls

- Peeking inflates false positives.
- Simpson’s paradox in segmented dashboards.
