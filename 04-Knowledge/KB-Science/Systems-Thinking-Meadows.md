# Systems thinking (Meadows)

## TL;DR

- **Stocks and flows** — inventory, users, cash.
- **Delays** cause oscillation; **leverage** often non-obvious.

## Source

- Meadows, *Thinking in Systems*.

## Mental Models Update — 2026-04-13

- **System 1 vs System 2** (Kahneman) — S1: fast/automatic; S2: slow/deliberate; most decisions use S1 with S2 override
- **Mental models function** — provide transparency + order; create context for complex problem-solving
- **Leverage points** — changing information flows > changing parameters; rules > physical structure
- **Feedback loop types** — reinforcing (amplify) vs balancing (stabilize); delays cause oscillation
- Source: [The Decision Lab](https://thedecisionlab.com/reference-guide/philosophy/system-1-and-system-2-thinking), [Systems Thinker](https://thesystemsthinker.com/what-are-mental-models/)
