# Growth & launch toolkit

> [!warning] **Verify pricing and ToS.** ASO tools, store policies, and marketing APIs change frequently.

---

## App Store Optimization (ASO)

| Tool | What it does | Free / entry notes |
|------|--------------|---------------------|
| [AppFollow](https://appfollow.io/pricing) | Keywords, reviews, competitors | Free/trial tiers — confirm |
| [Sensor Tower](https://sensortower.com/) | Market intel, ASO | Often paid; limited free insights |
| [AppTweak](https://www.apptweak.com/) | ASO + ad intel | Enterprise-leaning — verify trials |
| **Store consoles** | Apple App Analytics, Play Console | Free with developer accounts — primary data |

> [!tip] Start with **first-party** store analytics before paying for third-party ASO.

## Landing page & marketing site

| Resource | What it does | When to use |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| [Tailwind UI](https://tailwindui.com/) (paid) / [Headless UI](https://headlessui.com/) (free) | Components | Fast polished landing sections |
| [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) | Host static/marketing | BRUH landing pattern |
| **Examples** | Study crisp app landings (Linear, Notion mobile) | Copy structure, not assets |

| Tool | What it does | When to use |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| [PostHog](https://posthog.com/) | Funnels, retention, flags | EU instance for compliance — see [[Free Services & SaaS Directory]] |

## Beta, feedback & launch day

| Resource | What it does | When to use |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| **TestFlight** / **Google Play internal testing** | Distribute betas | Pre-launch crashes + UX |
| [Product Hunt](https://www.producthunt.com/) | Launch visibility | Prepare maker story + assets |
| **Discord** | Community + support | Early adopters — link from app settings |

## Share cards, OG images & deep links

| Resource | What it does | When to use |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| [Vercel OG Image](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/functions/edge-functions/og-image-generation) / `@vercel/og` | Dynamic OG images | Social previews for share URLs |
| **BRUH** | `landing/`, AASA, `assetlinks.json` | Canonical deep link setup — see vault **Deep Links & PWA** |
| **Branch / Firebase Dynamic Links** (ecosystem) | Deferred deep linking | Evaluate cost/complexity vs native App Links |

## Community building (lightweight)

| Tactic | Notes |
|--------|--------|
| **Founder-led changelog** | Short video or post per release — builds trust |
| **Power users** | Early Discord role + direct feedback channel |
| **Referral hooks** | Only after core loop is solid — avoid premature growth hacks |

## Legal & compliance (generators — verify with counsel)

| Resource | What it does | Caveat |
|----------|--------------|--------|
| **Privacy policy generators** | First draft | **Not** legal advice — review for GDPR/CCPA |
| **Terms / EULA templates** | App Store requirement baseline | Match actual data practices |
| **Cookie banners** | Web landing | Needed if non-essential cookies/trackers |

> [!note] Mobile apps still need accurate **privacy nutrition labels** and in-product disclosures.
