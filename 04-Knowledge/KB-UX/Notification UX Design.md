# Notification UX design

---

## Permission timing

| Early ask | Contextual ask |
|-----------|----------------|
| Low opt-in | **After** user sees value (“Get notified when someone replies”) |

---

## Types

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| **Transactional** | Reply to your post — high relevance |
| **Digest** | Weekly summary — lower frequency |
| **Marketing** | Separate opt-in where regulations require |

---

## Grouping

- **Collapse** same-type events (“5 people liked your post”)
- **Thread** conversation notifications

---

## Quiet hours & locale

- Respect timezone; allow user **mute** windows

---

## Deep link

- Tap → correct **screen** + **highlight** target entity
