# Social app engagement patterns

---

## Core loops

| Loop | Elements |
|------|----------|
| **Creation** | Low friction composer, templates, remix |
| **Feedback** | Likes, replies, notifications — timely but not spammy |
| **Distribution** | Share card, deep link open → install path |

Project: [[Share Card System]] · [[Deep Links & PWA]].

## Feed mechanics

- **Freshness** — pull-to-refresh + subtle “new posts” chip ([[Feed & Infinite Scroll Patterns]])
- **Ranking** — explain “why” if non-chronological (trust)

---

## Retention hooks

- **Streaks / challenges** — clear rules; avoid dark patterns
