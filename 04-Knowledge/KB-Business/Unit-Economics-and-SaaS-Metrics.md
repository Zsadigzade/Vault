# Unit economics and SaaS metrics

## TL;DR

- **CAC:** sales+marketing cost / new customers (define window).
- **LTV:** ARPA × gross margin × lifetime (or 1/churn if steady).
- **Payback:** CAC / (ARPA × gross margin).

## Sanity checks

- LTV:CAC commonly cited >3 — not universal law.
- High churn makes LTV fragile.
