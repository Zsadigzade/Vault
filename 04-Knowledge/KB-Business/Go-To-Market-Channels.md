# Go-to-market channels

## TL;DR

- **PLG:** product qualifies users; funnel instrumentation critical.
- **Sales-assist:** human for expansion / compliance.
- **Partner:** shared economics + clear ICP.

## Fit questions

- ACV vs CAC realistic?

## Indie App Marketing — 2026-04-14

- **10 proven plays** (zero/low budget): directory submissions, SEO-optimised listicles, community loops, timed launch plays — [rapidappstore.com 2026](https://rapidappstore.com/blog/indie-app-marketing-strategies)
- **TikTok content** for app demos working well for indie devs in 2025 (r/androiddev); open dedicated account per app
- **Listicle strategy**: create "Best X apps" content → share on social + communities → run small paid boost after organic validation
- **Time paid campaigns** after organic signals confirm messaging resonates; never run cold paid to unvalidated copy
