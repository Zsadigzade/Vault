# Business Model Canvas

## TL;DR

- One page; iterate fast; pair with **experiments** not slides.
- **Fit check:** value prop ↔ segment ↔ channel ↔ revenue stream — must be internally consistent.
- Fill right side first (customer-facing), then left side (operations to deliver).

## The 9 blocks

The canvas has two sides: **customer-facing (right)** and **operational (left)**, linked by the **Value Proposition** in the center.

### Right side — customer-facing

| Block | Question | Examples |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Customer Segments** | Who are we creating value for? | Mass market, niche, multi-sided, B2B |
| **Value Propositions** | What problem do we solve? What job are we hired for? | Speed, cost, design, access, risk reduction |
| **Channels** | How do we reach and deliver to segments? | Direct, web, retail, partner, app store |
| **Customer Relationships** | How do we acquire, retain, and grow customers? | Self-serve, community, dedicated support, automated |
| **Revenue Streams** | How do we capture value? | Subscription, transaction, licensing, advertising, freemium upgrade |

### Left side — operational

| Block | Question | Examples |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Key Resources** | What assets are essential? | IP, brand, data, platform, people, capital |
| **Key Activities** | What must we do to deliver the VP? | Engineering, content creation, sales, logistics |
| **Key Partnerships** | Who helps us scale? | Suppliers, co-creators, distributors, platform owners |
| **Cost Structure** | What are the major costs? | Fixed vs variable; cost-driven vs value-driven |


## Fit check (3 must be consistent)

1. **Value Prop ↔ Segment fit:** Does the VP solve a real, felt pain for this segment?
2. **Channel ↔ Segment fit:** Does the channel actually reach this segment at acceptable CAC?
3. **Revenue ↔ Value Prop fit:** Is the pricing model aligned with the value delivered?

If all three fit → product-market fit is plausible. If any don't → redesign before scaling.

---

## Common failure modes

| Pattern | What's wrong |
|---------|-------------|
| VP says "everyone" | No segment → no focus → poor conversion |
| Revenue model doesn't match VP | "We save you time" but charge per seat not per hour saved |
| Left side not costed | Canvas looks great until you model the cost structure |
| One-sided platform with no bootstrapping plan | Chicken-and-egg unsolved |
