# Competitive positioning

## TL;DR

- **Frame of reference:** category you replace or join.
- **POV:** 1–2 proof points (speed, cost, compliance, delight).

## Outputs

- Positioning statement template: For [segment], who [JTBD], [product] is [category] that [benefit] unlike [alt].
