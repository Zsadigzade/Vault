# PMF and discovery

## TL;DR

- **Mom Test:** ask about past behavior, not hypotheticals.
- **Signals:** organic pull, retention, willingness to pay, referrals.

## Interview focus

- What did you try last time?
- What sucked?
- What did it cost (time/money)?

## Source

- Fitzpatrick, *The Mom Test*; Ries, *Lean Startup* (build-measure-learn).

## PMF Signals — 2026-04-13

- **Bessemer AI PMF playbook** — track activation rate, retention cohorts, NPS; AI founders should look for "pull" not "push" adoption
- **First Round PMF levels** — sales-led B2B: reverse-engineered signals; measure pipeline velocity + deal size growth
- **Currency + locale pricing** — monitor exchange rate changes as proxy for international PMF; localize early if signal strong
- **Early UX metrics** — time-to-value < 5 min = strong signal; users returning without prompting = PMF indicator
- Source: [Bessemer BVP](https://www.bvp.com/atlas/mastering-product-market-fit-a-detailed-playbook-for-ai-founders), [First Round levels](https://www.firstround.com/levels)
