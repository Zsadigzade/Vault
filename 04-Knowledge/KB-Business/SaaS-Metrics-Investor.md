# SaaS metrics (investor lens)

## TL;DR

- **ARR/MRR:** contracted recurring; define exclusions (services, one-time).
- **Churn:** logo vs revenue; net retention (NRR) captures expansion.
- **Rule of 40:** growth rate + margin (definitions vary by stage).

## Caution

- Public benchmarks skew to VC-backed; sanity-check against your segment.
