# Pricing strategy

## TL;DR

- Anchor on **value metric** (seats, usage, outcome).
- **Packaging:** good/better/best reduces paradox of choice.
- **Tests:** change one lever; measure conversion + retention.

## Tactics

- Annual prepay discount vs cash flow
- Transparent vs opaque enterprise quotes

## Freemium Conversion Benchmarks — 2026-04-14

- **Typical freemium conversion**: 2–5% (Dropbox, Evernote, SurveyMonkey range)
- **Freemium → paid average**: ~15% per First Page Sage 2026 report (visitor→freemium is much lower)
- **Feature restriction**: limit key features (not core value) to push free→paid; Slack limits message history, Zapier limits zap runs
- **Mobile trend**: subscriptions now outperform paid downloads as primary model; in-app advertising revenue declining due to ATT/Privacy Sandbox privacy changes
- Subscription model favours retention focus over new acquisition volume
- Sources: [userpilot freemium guide](https://userpilot.com/blog/freemium-conversion-rate/), [First Page Sage 2026](https://firstpagesage.com/seo-blog/saas-freemium-conversion-rates/), [Adjust monetization trends Aug 2025](https://www.adjust.com/blog/app-monetization-trends/)
