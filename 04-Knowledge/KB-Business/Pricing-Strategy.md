# Pricing strategy

## TL;DR

- Anchor on **value metric** (seats, usage, outcome).
- **Packaging:** good/better/best reduces paradox of choice.
- **Tests:** change one lever; measure conversion + retention.

## Tactics

- Annual prepay discount vs cash flow
- Transparent vs opaque enterprise quotes
