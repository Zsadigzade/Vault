# Negotiation — BATNA / ZOPA

## TL;DR

- **BATNA:** plan before negotiating; improves walk-away clarity.
- **ZOPA:** only exists if parties’ ranges overlap.

## Prep checklist

- Interests (not positions)
- Options for mutual gain
- Legitimate criteria (market data)

## Source

- Fisher & Ury, *Getting to Yes*.
